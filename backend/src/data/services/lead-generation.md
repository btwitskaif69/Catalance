# Lead Generation Services - Client Intake Questionnaire

## Instructions for AI Assistant
Ask these questions ONE AT A TIME in order. Wait for each answer before proceeding.
Use [SUGGESTIONS: ...] for single-choice options and [MULTI_SELECT: ...] for multi-choice options.

## Questions (In Order)

1. What's your name?

2. What type of leads are you primarily looking for?
   [SUGGESTIONS: Phone call leads | Form submissions | WhatsApp or chat leads]

3. Who is your target audience?
   [SUGGESTIONS: Businesses (B2B) | Consumers (B2C) | A mix of both]

4. Which locations do you want to target for lead generation?
   [SUGGESTIONS: A specific city | A state or region | Pan-India]

5. If you selected "A specific city", which city should we target?

6. If you selected "A state or region", which state or region should we target?

7. How many leads do you expect per month?
   [SUGGESTIONS: Low volume | Medium volume | High volume]

8. Which channels should be used for lead generation? (Multiple)
   [MULTI_SELECT: Google | Meta | LinkedIn | Email | Third party tools]

9. What level of lead quality do you require?
   [SUGGESTIONS: Raw leads | Pre-qualified leads]

10. Do you currently use a CRM or lead tracking system?
    [SUGGESTIONS: Yes, a CRM is already in place | No]

11. How long do you want to run the lead generation campaigns?
    [SUGGESTIONS: Short-term | Medium-term | Long-term]

12. What is your monthly budget for lead generation?
    [SUGGESTIONS: Under ?15,000 / month | ?15,000 - ?30,000 / month | ?30,000 - ?60,000 / month | ?60,000 - ?1,00,000 / month | ?1,00,000 and above / month]
